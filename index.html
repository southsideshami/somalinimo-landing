Somalinimo: 65 Years of Independence

A Celebration of Identity, Unity & Legacy

Somalinimo isn’t just a word—it’s a pulse. A shared heartbeat connecting Somalis across oceans, generations, and borders. This event is a living tribute to who we are, where we come from, and where we’re going.

It’s a space to honour our roots, amplify our voices, and imagine the future of our people—with pride, purpose, and unity.

What to Expect:

  • Cultural Showcases: Traditional dance, poetry (gabayo), music, and storytelling
  • Panel Discussions: Somali excellence in business, art, politics, and diaspora impact
  • Food & Fashion: Authentic Somali cuisine and cultural clothing
  • Youth Spotlights: Celebrating young Somali talent and future leaders
  • Marketplace: Somali-owned brands, art, books, and crafts

This is more than a celebration. It’s a reclamation of narrative, a revival of pride, and a renewal of unity. Come as you are. Leave connected.
